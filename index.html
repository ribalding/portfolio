About Me

I was born and raised in Syracuse, NY. I graduated from Liverpool High School in 2007, after which I moved to Buffalo, NY to attend Buffalo State College. In 2012, I graduated with my BA in Philosophy and in 2015 I relocated to Portland, OR. My other main interest is music. I have been playing guitar, bass and drums for over ten years and have been in several groups. I also create music using computer software like FL Studios and Ableton. I believe the attraction to the logical components of philosophy, combined with my inclination toward creative pursuits like music are the two motivations which make coding appealing to me.
